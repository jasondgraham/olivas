Olivas Masonry is a third generation mason contractor established in 1975. We have been serving Southern California for over 35 years, and we continue to provide the best possible products to our customers. Our extensive knowledge and expertise allow us to provide cost effect, high quality solutions to meet your masonry needs. The cornerstone of our success is professionalism, dedication, and commitment to the successful completion of all our projects. Our main focus is Residential and Tract Housing masonry. We also do commercial masonry, city scape, and enhancement projects all over Southern California. We are a client-oriented company with a strong emphasis on developing, executing, and value engineering. Our extensive experience allows us to work in the collaborative approach, traditional plans and specs, as well as complete design-build solutions. We value a long standing tradition in the communities we serve, and will continue to provide our services for generations to come.
